Meet Our Founder: Rachel Gitome

Rachel Gitome is the visionary behind Jelani Afrika Insurance Agency. With over 15 years of experience in the insurance industry, Rachel founded the agency with a singular mission: to demystify insurance and make comprehensive protection accessible to everyone.

Her journey began as a passionate advocate for financial literacy, leading her to understand the critical role insurance plays in securing futures. Rachel's expertise spans across all major insurance categories, and she is renowned for her client-centric approach, always prioritizing transparency, empathy, and tailored solutions.

Under her leadership, Jelani Afrika has grown into a trusted name, built on the principles of integrity and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Rachel believes that insurance isn't just about policies; it's about building lasting relationships and empowering individuals and businesses to face tomorrow with confidence.
