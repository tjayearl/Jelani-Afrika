Home Insurance

Your home is more than just a building; it's your sanctuary. Our home insurance policies are designed to protect your biggest asset and the valuable possessions within it. From fire and theft to natural disasters, we provide the comprehensive coverage you need for total peace of mind.

Key Coverage Areas

  • Building Protection: Covers the structure of your home against damage from insured events.
  • Contents Cover: Protects your personal belongings, including furniture, electronics, and clothing.
  • Natural Disasters: Coverage for events like floods, storms, and earthquakes.
  • Theft & Burglary: Reimburses you for stolen items and covers damage caused during a break-in.
  • Liability Protection: Covers you if someone is injured on your property and you are found legally responsible.

Why Choose Our Home Insurance?

We offer flexible policies that can be customized to fit your specific needs and budget. Our claims team is dedicated to providing fast and fair settlements, helping you rebuild and recover without unnecessary delays. Protect your home and family with Jelani-Africa.
